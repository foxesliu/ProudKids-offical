WHO WE ARE & WHAT DO WE DO?

Proud Kids is a dynamic English education provider for children between 5-12 years old in China.

We are the first online English education company in China to have combined local English teachers with native English-speaking teachers in English teaching. Our curriculum design aligns with the local English education system.

We aim to provide high-quality and equal English learning opportunities to children all over China, empowering more children to speak English with authentic pronunciation. We do it right from the start and never compromise!

Proud Kids has incorporated an authentic English teaching system based on CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning) with China's new national curriculum for English, aiming to help children in China between the ages of 5-12 to learn authentic English more effectively.

Unlike other English education providers, we believe that children should not learn English from frequently changing teachers. Having the same teacher teaching for a certain period will result better teacher-student bond, relationship, students' learning experience and performance.
